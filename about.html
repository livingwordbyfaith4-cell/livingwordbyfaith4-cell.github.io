Our Story

Led by the Word, Walking by Faith

A testimony of spiritual warfare, absolute deliverance, and the unyielding power of Jesus Christ.

Living Word By Faith was born out of a profound, life-altering encounter with the reality of the spiritual realm. Years ago, during a severe season of spiritual attack, a terrifying manifestation occurred: a dark, shadowy silhouette—the classic entity known as the hat man—was seen standing motionless in the room, watching and waiting to instill fear and bondage.

In that exact moment of darkness, rather than yielding to panic, a supernatural anchor took hold. The absolute authority of Scripture rose up from within. Refusing to bow to intimidation, the battle was fought and won not by human strength, but by declaring the living Word of God. Total deliverance swept through the room, and the oppressive presence was completely shattered.

That pivotal night proved an unchanging truth: we do not wrestle against flesh and blood. True freedom, healing, and authority are found exclusively through living by the Word, by faith. This ministry exists today to hand you those exact spiritual weapons so you can walk completely free.
